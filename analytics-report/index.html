Insight

Volume ≠ Quality

Paid Social drives 55% of sessions but converts at only 0.57%. Direct and Organic Search convert at 3–4× that rate. If you're optimizing for revenue per session, focus on retention and lifetime value from high-quality channels—not just volume.
