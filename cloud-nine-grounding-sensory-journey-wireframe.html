Practical questions

What to know before it arrives.

What exactly is built into the Grounding Hoodie?

Removable plush squeeze tools sit inside the cuffs. A fixed silicone texture patch sits inside the kangaroo pocket.

How do I wash it?

Remove the cuff inserts. Machine wash the hoodie cold on a gentle cycle. Avoid bleach and fabric softener, then follow the current care label for drying before reinserting the cuff tools.

How heavy is it?

It uses 360 GSM heavyweight fabric and weighs approximately 1 kg. It is a substantial hoodie, not a medical device.

How does it fit?

It has a slightly oversized unisex fit in sizes XS–4XL. Choose your usual size for the intended relaxed fit.

Can I return it if the fit is wrong?

Eligible unworn, unwashed items with tags and original packaging can be returned within 30 days. Review the current return policy for complete conditions.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. Available shipping methods, costs and delivery estimates appear at checkout for your location.
